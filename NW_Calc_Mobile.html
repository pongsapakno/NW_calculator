📘 Standards Reference — Allowable Hoop Stress

σh = P × D / (2 × t)  ·  Barlow's Formula (ASME B31.8 §841.11)
Allowable:  σh ≤ F × E × T × SMYS

Location Class Description Design Factor F Max % SMYS
Class 1, Div 1 Open country, desert, mountain — very low population 0.80 80%

Class 2 Fringe areas of cities, industrial — 10–46 buildings/mile² 0.60 60%
Class 3 Suburban housing, shopping, schools — >46 buildings/mile² 0.50 50%

Standard Application Max Hoop Stress Notes

EN 1594 European gas transmission pipelines 72% SMYS Standard MRS approach (fd × SMYS)
Design Factors (ASME B31.8 §841.114)
F (Location) — Design factor based on Class Location (0.40–0.80)
E (Joint) — Longitudinal joint factor: Seamless = 1.0, ERW = 1.0, SAW = 1.0, Furnace Butt Weld = 0.60
T (Temp) — Temperature derating: ≤250°F (121°C) = 1.0 · 300°F = 0.967 · 350°F = 0.933 · 400°F = 0.900 · 450°F = 0.867
⚠️ Hydrostatic Test Pressure Requirement (ASME B31.8 §841.322)
Minimum test pressure = 1.25 × MOP (above ground) or 1.1 × MOP (offshore sub-sea)
Test pressure shall produce hoop stress ≥ 90% SMYS at the highest point of the section
Maximum test pressure shall not exceed 100% SMYS
